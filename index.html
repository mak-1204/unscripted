About Us

We Turn Nerves Into Power

Unscripted is NIT Trichy's official public speaking club — a Toastmasters-inspired community where engineers discover their voice. We believe every great idea deserves a confident speaker.

Through structured speech pathways, mentor-led guidance, and a community that cheers you on at every step, we transform hesitation into confidence and whispers into keynotes.
